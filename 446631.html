17 Aug 2017 02:34 in response to Immunotherapy

Hi there,

Welcome to the forum,

I am so sorry to hear about your cancer diagnosis.No matter how well prepared we are to hear this news it always seems to hit us like a bolt out of the blue.You will probably find that you experience a whole raft of different emotions in the coming weeks. Don't worry about these. They are a perfectly natural reaction to such devastating news.

Some people want to know exactly what their prognosis is, while others, like yourself , don't want to know. Neither way is wrong. What is good is your positive attitude to treatment. I hope that you can keep your positivity going and that you get a good outcome.

Please keep us informed as to how you get on. There is always someone here whenever you feel like talking.

Kind regards,

Jolamine xx
